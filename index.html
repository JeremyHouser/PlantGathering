d100 tables for scavenging plants

Broderick's method for nature scavenging is a nature check. 10-14 is mildly successful, 15-19 is successful, 20+ is very successful. This would mean that for a mild success, roll on the table with disadvantage, for a success, roll on the table to see what kind of plant you get, and for very successful roll with advantage.

In most cases, 100 will also count as Very Rare, Legendary should only be in one or two places around the world, so make sure the settingâ€™s right to get a Legendary plant.

The baseline recommendation according to the Dungeon Master's Guide is that the character discovers 1d6 + Wisdom Modifier pounds of food. I prefer to use the generic term "units" as recovering 11 pounds of Vortex doesn't work. I then reduce the amount found by a compounding 10% for each level of rarity above common. As always, you're free to use this however you wish, you don't even need to use the below tool, this is just how I do it.
